    Coaching and Equipping Pastors and Leaders

    Make Room provides multiple environments and resources for pastors and church leaders who want to grow their impact and leadership, build the local church, and develop their teams.

      Make Room Events

      Whiteboard Sessions

      Make Room Whiteboard Sessions provide an environment for pastors and church leaders to connect and learn from Pastor John Siebeling and The Life Church Team with practical teaching on topics such as defining church culture, delivering excellent weekend experiences, and more.

      Learn More >

      Cohort

      The Make Room Cohort is a small group of Lead Pastors and their spouses who lead growing, influential churches and want to commit to taking a year-long journey alongside Pastors John and Leslie Siebeling and The Life Church team to build something great for God.

      Learn More >

      International Events

      Make Room International Events provide an environment for pastors and church leaders to connect and learn from Pastor John Siebeling and The Life Church team with practical teachings on topics such as church growth, healthy culture, leadership development, and more.

      Learn More > 

      Other Events

      Throughout the year, The Life Church offers other events and conferences that are great for ministry leaders in your church to attend. These events provide great opportunities to grow spiritually and build relationships with other church leaders from across the country.

        About Pastor John

        John and Leslie Siebeling are the founders and Senior Pastors of The Life Church, a thriving, diverse body of believers committed to serving people, developing leaders, and impacting generations. With 25+ years of experience leading in ministry, John and Leslie consider it one of their greatest honors to pastor The Life Church and come alongside churches to invest in building the local church around the world.
